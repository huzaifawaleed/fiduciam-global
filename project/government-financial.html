Government Financial Management

Strengthening public financial management, governance, and accountability in the public sector

Overview

What we do in
Government Financial Management

Fiduciam Global Consulting provides specialized services for public sector financial management, governance, and accountability. We understand the unique challenges and constraints of the public sector — from political dynamics and regulatory complexity to capacity constraints and accountability requirements.

Our government finance practice brings together experts with deep public sector experience across Saudi Arabia and internationally. We support governments and public institutions in building more effective, transparent, and efficient financial management systems.

Public Financial Management

Strengthening budget formulation, execution, reporting, and audit processes in alignment with international public sector standards.

Budget Reform

Designing and implementing program-based budgeting, medium-term fiscal frameworks, and performance budgeting systems.

🏦

Treasury Operations

Modernizing treasury functions including cash management, debt management, and government banking arrangements.

Audit & Accountability

Strengthening internal audit functions, financial controls, and accountability frameworks across government entities.
